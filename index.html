WAJUG is your local, Walloon, user group related to "all things software development".
All this in a fun, friendly and open way, characteristic from the Walloon folks.

Jargons

Come and joins us to learn and discuss topics such as programming languages of all kinds, e.g.

  • Java
  • .Net
  • Groovy
  • Scala
  • JavaScript
  • PHP

Agile

Learn how to apply your skills in the most efficient way, by improving your software development process using the Agile methodology.

  • Scrum
  • Lean
  • Kanban
  • ...

Platforms

  • Cloud computing, IAAS, PAAS, SAAS
  • GAE, Amazon, ...

Tooling

How to integrate these tools, what they are, why should I embrace them?

  • Version Control Systems (Git, Mercurial, Subversion)
  • Continuous Integration (Jenkins, etc.)
  • ...

Your stuff

But WAJUG is more than this.
It is your user group, dedicated to address your topics of interest. As such, feel free to post your suggestions through the WAJUG Facebook page and/or through Twitter.

Similarly, if you are interested in presenting a talk, don't hesitate and let us know.

Interested? Great! Here's what you can do:

  • Attend our next events. We'll be announcing the agenda as soon as we have enough people.
  • Follow us on Twitter and Facebook.
  • Spread the word around you, recommend WAJUG to your friends and colleagues.
  • And if you dare to help, we're looking for sponsors/partners and meeting rooms in and around Liege.
